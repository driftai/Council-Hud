## Architectural Philosophy:
- **Robust, Not Rigid**: Systems must assume the network will fail and build self-healing loops (The Iron Link).
- **Armor-Less Design**: Move away from fragile "Handshakes" toward enterprise-grade tunnels (Cloudflare).
- **Unified Ingress**: One HUD should support unlimited data streams (Multiplexing).
